Info

At Matrix University, we welcome forward-thinking, curious, and driven individuals who are ready to shape the future. Our admissions process is designed to identify students with strong academic foundations, leadership potential, and a passion for innovation.


Program requirements

Completed high school or equivalent qualification (e.g., SPM, STPM, A-Levels, UEC, IGCSE, IB, or national equivalent)
Minimum of 5 credits including English and Mathematics

Minimum CGPA or grade:
STPM: CGPA 2.50 and above with passes in at least two relevant subjects
A-Levels: At least 2 passes in relevant subjects
Foundation/Matriculation: Minimum CGPA of 2.00

English Language Proficiency (for non-native English speakers):
MUET Band 3 / IELTS 6.0 / TOEFL iBT 78 / Equivalent
